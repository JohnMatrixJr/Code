About Us

We are a Southampton-based IT training provider that aims to meet all your staff IT training requirements delivering training at a tranquil and stunning setting in the New Forest or at your offices.

We run regular scheduled courses in many software applications. However, if you require training in an application we don't have listed, it's worth asking!

Over 20 years background experience in delivering IT solutions and training.

From a delegate needing the computer basics, to advanced Excel, database design, Project Management, or Adobe product training, we have a vast knowledge base which means we can be put together a course for whatever you need.

We offer a flexible approach and friendly professional manner and have only ever received positive feedback. The training packages are flexible, top quality and value for money.
